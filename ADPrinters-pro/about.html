Managing Director Message

A.D .Printers (PVT) Ltd comprises a team of dedicated forces who identify the importance of satisfying customer which is why we have been successful of our foundation. Customer satisfaction is the key to success, who approaches us, is treated with dignity Big or Small, are treated equally. We satisfy the customers who need even a single box.
Formally known as A.D.Printers with a small offset company, we are now large scale offset and flexo printing company. Now,We own state of the art technology for the offset and flexo printing. A well- equipped printing outfit, employing over 80 employees and processing over 5 containers of paper and board into printed a month. We have a long established source of dedicated material suppliers and outsource services providers. I all ways believe in achieving quality through good practice and to achieve this, quality is not in an accident.
A.D.Printers has become a trusted printing for corporate client as Hayley’s Group, Ceylon Biscuit, Mas Holding, Mabrock Teas, Superfine Teas, and Hansel Lanka etc... Company serves a prestigious clientele local and overseas covering internationally brands.
Human resource is the basic driving force of any organization. We are capable of doing our production with high quality by using our man power handled by an energetic team of well qualified staff in the printing industry. We trust that our one of key to success secret is our team work. We maintain the quality as well as quantity required by the customer at a short period of time
This is only principal information about us to enable you give us an opportunity to render our service. We build lasting relationship through services based on honestly, reliability, professionalism and ethical business practice.
I request you, our customer, to go through this company profile of our establishment which tells you what we are, for a better understanding and for lasting relationship between us.

Thank You
Ajith Perera
